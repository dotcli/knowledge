vvvv

vvvv

Interface

vvvv relies heavily on shortcuts.

Close a window: CTRL+W

Hide a window: ALT+3

Open a patch: CTRL+O

Create new patch: CTRL+P

Misc

There's a nuget.exe that comes with vvvv. For version vvvv_50beta38.1_x64, it's located at: lib\packs\nuget.exe
