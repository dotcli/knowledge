Memex is my public bookmarks, built with Hamish MacDonald's wonderful tool.

Bookmarks in the memex can be filtered by tags, and type of media.

This wiki interacts with the memex; look for the #tag links to find bookmarks related to a topic.

Memex Visualized

By running the memex bookmarks through t-SNE, we get a visualization of how bookmarks relate to each other.
